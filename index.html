Sami Halaseh

I am a math PhD student in LAAS-CNRS Toulouse and the University of Konstanz. I am part of the TENORS network. My research interests are polynomial optimization, real algebraic geometry and tropical geometry. My supervisors are Mateusz Skomra, Victor Magron (Toulouse) and Markus Schweighofer (Konstanz).

Email: shalaseh at laas.fr

About

I grew up in Jordan and Canada. I did my Bachelors degree at the University of Toronto, and my Masters degree at the Free University in Berlin. My masters thesis was in tropical geometry, specifically in tropical convexity with an application to scheduling problems in combinatorial optimization [pdf].
